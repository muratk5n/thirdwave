The Math for Greenhouse Effect and Global Warming

I like how the video covers how the science was developed and the steps its inventors took, their thoughts to get there.

Note: Water vapor is effectively a greenhouse gas, and itself a product of greenhouse effect. So you get into a doom-loop, warmer air can hold more vapor which in turn acts as a greenhouse gas, helping air trap more heat. A similar loop exists for carbon which is trapped in permafrost, as it melts, it accelerates carbon release.
