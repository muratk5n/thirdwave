Example, 490 polled say candidate A, 470 say candidate B, out of 1000 percentages say A is ahead 49% vs 47%. Compute conf

def d(k,n): m=k/n; return m,1.96*np.sqrt(m*(1-m)/n)

n = 1000.; a=490; b=470
m,t = d(a,n)
print (np.round([m-t,m+t],2))
m,t = d(b,n)
print (np.round([m-t,m+t],2))
